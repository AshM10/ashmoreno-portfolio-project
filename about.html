Hi I am Ash Moreno

Frontend Developer, based in Austin, Texas.

As a kid, I have always been fascinated with computer games. All the cool characters, solving each challenge, gaining access to the next levels, finishing the entire game on hard mode, competing with other players, and just admiring how beautiful a game was built; I became addicted to computer games and I wanted to play my whole life.
It was hard for me coming from a poor family, I do not have access to all the games that I love. I remember saving up my lunch money so I can go to computer rentals, top-up my character, and play hard to become one of the strongest players in the game. I would also hang out with friends and cousins who had the newest consoles, play computer games with them, and beat them.
I knew then that I would want to be a game developer! Combining my love for art, making characters for a game, and programming a different game that would challenge me each time.
My dream was cut short when I wasn’t accepted in by my college to pursue Computer Science. It was hard to be self-taught back then. I had to go on with my life and continue studying for a different career. The path I took, taught me a lot of things about customer service. I used to be a loner, but I became sociable, and I gained a lot of friends. Suddenly it wasn’t just the computer I get along with! This path made me the person I am today, and I wouldn’t change anything about it.
Fast forward to now, I am able to finish coding workshops through SheCodes! I learned a lot about Front-end and React Development through their online workshops. I know this is just the beginning. I will continue learning, so I can become a Fullstack Developer, and eventually a Game Developer!
